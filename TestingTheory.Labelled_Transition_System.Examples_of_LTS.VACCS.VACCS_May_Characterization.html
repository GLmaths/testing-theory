The May preorder is trace inclusion, for VACCS

VACCS is only a feedback LTS (VACCS_gLtsOBAFB, VACCS_Instance.v) — unlike VCCS, proc is not itself a forwarder — so the characterisation goes through equivalence_ti_fb on the lifted pairs (p, ∅ : MO A), exactly as VACCS_Must_Characterization.v's corollaries all read on p ▷ ∅. The three Prop_of_Inter obligations (Interaction_between_VACCS_and_MB, Interaction_between_FW_VACCS_and_VACCS, Interaction_between_parallel_VACCS, all in VACCS_Instance.v) are already in scope as global instances, self-testing with T := proc.

Notation "p ᴠᴀᴄᴄꜱ⊑ₘₐᵧ q" := (p ⊑ₘₐᵧ q) (at level 70).
